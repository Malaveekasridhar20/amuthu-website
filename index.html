— FAQ —

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our treatments and clinic.

What are your clinic timings?

We are open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM, and on Sundays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM.

Do you offer root canal treatments, and are they painful?

Yes, we specialize in Micro-Surgical Root Canals. Using modern local anesthesia and advanced rotary endodontics, the procedure is virtually painless and highly comfortable.

How often should I visit the dentist for a check-up and cleaning?

We recommend a routine dental check-up and professional teeth cleaning every 6 months to prevent dental issues and maintain optimal oral health.

Do you offer orthodontic treatments like clear aligners and braces?

Yes, we offer clear aligners (including Invisalign) and all types of braces (metal, ceramic, self-ligating) to correct misaligned teeth for patients of all ages.

What digital technologies do you use at your clinic?

Our clinic is equipped with state-of-the-art technologies including High-Magnification Dental Loupes, Intra-Oral 3D Scanners, and digital imaging systems for highly precise diagnosis and treatment.
