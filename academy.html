Workshop in Rotary Endodontics

Intensive 15-Day One-to-One Clinical Mentorship & Hands-on Training

Duration: 15 Days
Mentorship: 1-on-1 Only
Focus: Rotary Endodontics
CURRICULUM

What You'll Master

Designed to transition dentists from manual instrumentation to advanced rotary techniques with absolute confidence.

01

Case Selection, Diagnosis & Treatment Planning

Understanding patient symptoms, correct diagnosis workflows, and case planning.

  • Endodontic diagnosis
  • Clinical examination
  • Radiographic interpretation
  • Pulp and periapical diagnosis
  • Case selection
  • Treatment planning
  • Case difficulty assessment
02

Fundamentals of Rotary Endodontics

Transitioning from hand files to rotary endodontic instruments with precision.

  • Access cavity preparation
  • Working length determination
  • Canal scouting
  • Glide path preparation
  • Introduction to rotary file systems
  • Instrument selection and sequence
  • Safe rotary instrumentation principles
03

Cleaning, Shaping & Obturation

Mastering irrigation, shaping sequences, and obturation for optimal canal sealing.

  • Irrigation techniques and activation protocols
  • Obturation methods & sealer selection
  • Single-visit vs. Multi-visit RCT strategies
04

Advanced Endodontics

Managing complex root canals, clinical troubleshooting, and procedural tips.

  • Management of difficult & curved canals
  • Common mistakes & complication management
  • Clinical tips, tricks, and evidence-based protocols
PROGRAM BENEFITS

Why Join Our Program?

Our mentorship is tailored around practical clinical skills, ensuring you acquire hands-on confidence, not just theory.

  • Individual One-to-One Mentoring: Personalized clinical guidance directly from experienced endodontists.
  • 15 Days of Personalized Clinical Training: Extensive timeline for procedural step mastery.
  • Live Patient Case Exposure: Gain real clinical experience on active patients.
  • Hands-on Practice under Expert Guidance: Step-by-step supervision to build confidence.
  • Modern Endodontic Instruments: Training with advanced rotary motors and apex locators.

BDS Graduates

General Practitioners

Skill Upgraders
