About Mr. Balaji Rajendren

Mr. Balaji Rajendren is a Dental Hygienist, Dental prosthetist and Ceramist with over 26+ years of experience. He is a skilled oral healthcare professional dedicated to promoting oral health through preventive care and the restoration of missing teeth. With expertise in both dental hygiene and prosthetic rehabilitation, he plays a vital role in helping patients achieve healthy, functional, and confident smiles.

His responsibilities include providing professional teeth cleaning, removing plaque and tartar, assessing gum health, educating patients on proper oral hygiene practices, and assisting in the prevention and management of oral diseases. In addition, he specializes in the design, fabrication, fitting, adjustment, repair, and maintenance of complete and partial dentures, ensuring optimal comfort, function, aesthetics, and speech for patients with missing teeth.

Committed to clinical excellence, patient comfort, and evidence-based care, he works collaboratively with dentists and the dental team to deliver comprehensive, personalized treatment that enhances both oral health and overall quality of life.
